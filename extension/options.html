About BooksInGC

The BooksInGC browser extension lets you easily determine the availability of booksin the Green Commons as you browse Amazon, Goodreads, and Barnes & Noble.

Preferences

You can change these options at any time from the extensions/add-ons menu or by clicking 'options & support' in the BooksInDC search box.
